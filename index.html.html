Welcome to Our Table

Our Story

Casimiro Pastaria introduces a fresh and creative twist through its unique “Pasta on a Stick” concept of portable snack inspired by Filipino street foods. Instead of serving pasta in the usual bowls or plates, the business transforms classic dishes into convenient, handheld skewers using pasta shapes like penne, and baked varieties.
